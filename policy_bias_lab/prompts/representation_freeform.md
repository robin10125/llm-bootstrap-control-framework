REPRESENTATION = FREE-FORM. Each phase sub-prior is a list of `channels`. A channel is {actuators:[<actuator names or semantic groups>], expr:'<expression>'}. The expr is the normalized mean-shift (-1..1) for those actuators, an arithmetic function of the SIGNALS above using only: + - * / , clip(x,lo,hi), sigmoid(x), min, max, abs, exp, and single comparisons (a<b).
